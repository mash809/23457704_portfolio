During a scheduled lab, I participated in a discussion with my friends regarding the reported exploitation of Cisco SD-WAN appliances. We discussed how attackers were targeting SD-WAN infrastructure, how the vulnerability could allow authentication bypass and root-level access, and why unpatched network systems can create serious security risks for organisations. We also discussed the importance of timely software updates, active monitoring, and threat detection in reducing the chance of compromise.
