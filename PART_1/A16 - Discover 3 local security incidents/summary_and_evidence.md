Upon research, I found three local Western Australia security incidents.

1. University of Western Australia data breach
  - In August 2025, UWA suffered a cyber attack that exposed thousands of staff and student passwords. The university locked affected accounts and reset passwords as a precaution.

https://www.abc.net.au/news/2025-08-11/university-of-western-australia-uwa-suffers-major-data-breach/105636074

2. Legal Practice Board of Western Australia cyber incident
  - The Legal Practice Board of WA reported a cyber incident in late May 2025 that forced some systems, including online services, offline. The organisation continued some services manually while investigating and restoring systems.

https://www.lpbwa.org.au/cyber-incident

3. Microsoft 365 security weaknesses in WA state entities
  - In March 2026, the WA Auditor General reported weaknesses in how seven state entities managed Microsoft 365 security controls. The report showed that poor configuration and weak controls can increase the risk of compromise in government environments.

[https://audit.wa.gov.au/reports-and-publications/reports/microsoft-365-security-controls-state-entities/
