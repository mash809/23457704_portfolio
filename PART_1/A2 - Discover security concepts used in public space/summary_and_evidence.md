In public spaces, I observed a range of security concepts such as CCTV cameras, public Wi-Fi login systems, police or security presence, and anti-theft gates. These examples show how public areas use a combination of monitoring, restricted access, and theft prevention methods to improve safety and reduce the risk of crime or unauthorised activity.
