I taught my mother about phishing and scams by sending her a message explaining how scammers often pretend to be banks, delivery companies, government agencies, or even family members in order to steal money or personal information. I explained the dangers of clicking suspicious links, sharing OTP codes or passwords, and responding to urgent-looking messages without verification. This activity was useful because it focused on practical cyber safety in an everyday context. I have attached the chat as evidence.
