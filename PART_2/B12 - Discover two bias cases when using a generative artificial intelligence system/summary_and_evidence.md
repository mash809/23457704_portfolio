I researched two bias cases using the article 5 Real-life Examples of AI Bias. 

One case showed that artificial intelligence risk-prediction systems could display racial and religious bias by being more likely to recommend police action for African American or Muslim individuals in simulated crisis situations. 

Another case showed gender bias in online advertising, where high-paying job advertisements were shown far more often to men than to women. These examples show that artificial intelligence systems can reflect unfair patterns from the data they learn from and may reinforce discrimination if left unchecked.
