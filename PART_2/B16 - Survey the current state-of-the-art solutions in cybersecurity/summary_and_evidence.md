I surveyed the current state-of-the-art solutions in cybersecurity using the article The Current Cybersecurity Landscape, Explained. The article showed that modern cybersecurity solutions increasingly focus on artificial intelligence-driven threat detection and response, automated phishing and malware detection, behavioural analytics for insider threat detection, security automation such as patch management and vulnerability scanning, and fraud prevention in financial transactions. It also highlighted that organisations are investing strongly in identity and access management, network security, and data privacy, while many enterprises now prefer unified security platforms with centralised dashboards, open application programming interfaces, and modular architectures to reduce tool sprawl and improve incident response. These trends show that current cybersecurity solutions are becoming more intelligent, automated, and integrated across the enterprise.
